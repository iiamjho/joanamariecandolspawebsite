Spa Etiquette

1. RING

Call ahead to book an appointment to ensure hassle-free service.

2. M.D

Inform us if you are pregnant or have any medical conditions we need to be aware of prior to the service.
The Regenerate Wellness Spa reserves the right to refuse treatment at its discretion.

3. TWO TIME

We need to know 2 things:
one: your time of arrival, and
two: the time you intend to start your treatment. Some guests like to take a few moments in between to enjoy our facilities.

4. CLOCK COURTESY

We highly appreciate your arrival thirty minutes prior to your appointment to ensure a hassle-free experience. A
fifteen-minute grace period is likewise provided, however please understand that delays cut into your treatment time and
your slot may be given to walk-in guests.

5. CANCELLATION

Our trained professionals are here to provide the best service possible. As a courtesy to them, please call at least three hours
before if you are unable to make it.

6. QUIET

The Spa Wellness is a haven of tranquility. We request that you lower your voice when talking and mobile phones on silent
mode as not to disturb the other guests. Smoking is strictly prohibited.

7. NO VALUABLES

Do secure your belongings. Management accepts no responsibility for any loss or damage.

8. TELL US

Let us know how we did. Feedback from our trusted guests are valuable to us.
